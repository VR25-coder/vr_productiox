About Me

I'm a cinematographer and video editor focused on cinematic commercials, documentary narratives and digital-first campaigns.

I help brands, institutions and creators turn raw footage into clear, emotionally driven stories. From look development and pacing to colour and sound, I care about how each cut feels and how the overall film supports the message. Alongside client work, I'm pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science at G.H. Raisoni College of Engineering (GHRCE), which gives me a strong technical base in areas such as cybersecurity and systems. I also work part-time as a Video Editor at Entrance Zone (Vatsala Academic), managing end-to-end production for education-focused content at scale.
